Synopsis

The perfect classic RPG. Eternal Requiem is designed to use every pixel effectively. Its classic gameplay and feel is designed to bring back the best of your childhood while incorporating innovative new mechanics. Designed for all ages ER is guarenteed not to dissapoint.

Classes

Soldier

(Kyle)

The mightiest of all heroes begin their journey as soldiers. Specializing in attack power and defense, soldiers can deal massive damage and taken more melee hits than the average hero.

Rogue

(Kyle)

Light on their feet, rogues specialize in quick, precise, and powerful attacks. By performing combo attacks rogues can perform incredible amounts of damage. Their hide ability allows them to recuperate after a big combo attack.

Mage

(Kyle)

Although they are weak to physical attacks, a mages high resistance and power makes them a radical threat on the battlefield. Mages perform best by chaining together spells and focusing on elemental precision in order to get the advantage in combat.

Guard

(Garrett)

Alike to soldiers, guards have incredibly high defenses. Their main role is endure through the harshest attacks while still being able to retaliate for considerable damage.
